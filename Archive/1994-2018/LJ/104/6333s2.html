Problem with 1.9 and chroot

You may have problems running ftp-proxy chrooted, or at least I have. Even using the chroot-jail functionality in the ftp-proxy startup script, I've had odd results with ftp-proxy's chroot support.

In my tests with version 1.9, ftp-proxy refuses to start, returning the error “Can't determine Group-ID to use”. In my tests with version 1.7 this wasn't a problem, but in transparent mode, ftp-proxy couldn't do DNS resolution properly. You may have better results than I did.

When in doubt, assistance is available in the form of a dedicated mailing list maintained by SuSE. To subscribe, send an empty e-mail to proxy-suite-subscribe@suse.com.
