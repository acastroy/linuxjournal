Leviathan can be reached through the URL's http://leviathan.tamu.edu/, gopher://leviathan.tamu.edu, and ftp://leviathan.tamu.edu.

We chose the name Leviathan to suggest largeness and power. My other Linux machines are named Behemoth, Gargantua and Pantagruel. Visitors are often surprised to meet Leviathan in the flesh. They see a standard 80486/33 PC with 16 MB memory, two small IDE drives, and a network card. Conspicuously absent are a keyboard and monitor. They often ask, almost disappointedly, “Is that all there is?” I then explain that all of Leviathan's software, including the operating system, is “priceless”.
