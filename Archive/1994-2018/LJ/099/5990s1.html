Ericsson Joins the Open Source Development Lab

Ericsson has joined the Open Source Development Lab (OSDL) working with other members to develop feature roadmaps to enable Linux for the enterprise and telecommunications market segments. As a member of OSDL, Ericsson will be focusing its efforts within the Carrier-Grade Linux Working Group with other members gathering requirements, establishing roadmaps, publishing specifications, defining architectures and sponsoring an open-source project needed to achieve a true, carrier-grade Linux. The Open Systems Lab at Ericsson Research will be representing Ericsson. The DSI Project is one of several Ericsson projects to be contributed to the Open Source community.
