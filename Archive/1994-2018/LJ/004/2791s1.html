The U.S. Chapter of DECUS, the Digital Equipment Computer Users Society, is an Association of Information Technology professionals interested in the products, services, and technologies of Digital Equipment Corpora-tion and related vendors. The Association's purpose is to promote the unimpeded ex-change of information, with the goal of helping its members and their organizations to be more successful. The Chapter provides members with means to enhance their professional development, forums for technical training, mechanisms for obtaining up-to-date information, advocacy programs, and opportunities for informal disclosure and interaction with professional colleagues of like interests.

Membership in the DECUS U.S. Chapter is free and is granted on an individual basis.
