Resources

Main VNC home page at ORL: http://www.orl.co.uk/vnc/

On-line documentation page for VNC servers and viewers: http://www.orl.co.uk/vnc/docs.html

VNC download page for precompiled binaries, source and documentation: http://www.orl.co.uk/vnc/download.htm

Various contributed VNC viewer ports plus hints on how to get the viewer compiled and working on your favorite OS for which ORL does not supply a binary: http://www.orl.co.uk/vnc/contribs.html

To subscribe to the VNC mailing list, send e-mail to vnc-list-request@orl.co.uk with the text “subscribe vnc-list” in the body of the message.
