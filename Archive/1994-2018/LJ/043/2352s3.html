Resources

The IP Masquerade mailing list is the primary resource for information and getting help on the topic. To subscribe to the list, send a mail message to masq-request@indyramp.com with the single word subscribe as the subject. The mail list archive is available at http://www.indyramp.com/lists/masq.

Information via web sites is available from:

  • http://www.wwonline.com/~achau/ipmasq/
  • http:/www.indyramp.com/masq/

The MINI HOW-TO and FAQ are also available from the above web sites.
