Here is what another (slightly more) famous person had to say about being interviewed electronically. While promoting his new movie “Bullets Over Broadway” online to the 800 or so people logged in to America Online, Woody Allen said, “It was a very interesting and eerie experience to be interviewed electronically. I hope that the exposure to the technology will not make me sterile.”
