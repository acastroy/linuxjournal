New at LJ

In order to better serve our customers, we have made some changes at Linux Journal.

You can place subscription orders and renewals by phone 24 hours/day. We have added an answering service that covers off-hour calls. Call 1 888 66 LINUX in the US or Canada toll free or +1 206 781 9566 anywhere else on the planet. You can still FAX +1 206 781 7191 or e-mail info@linuxjournal.com any time.

While the answering service cannot tell you your subscription status, our web page can. Armed with the information from your subscription label, go to http://www.linuxjournal.com/subinquiry.html.

Some international subscribers don't have a credit card or easy access to US funds. To address this problem, we are adding subscription resellers all over the world. We currently have over 40 offices in 25 countries. Check out http://www.linuxjournal.com/sublj.html.

Finally, we have totally updated our Linux Resources pages (http://www.linuxresources.com/). Changes include a new look, a mirror of the Linux Documentation Project and more business-oriented material. Take a look and let us know what you think. We want to be your definitive source of information about Linux.
