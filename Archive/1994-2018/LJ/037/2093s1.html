MkLinux Compatibility List

Supported Machines:
Power Macintosh 61xx, 71xx, 81xx, Power Computing 100 & 120
Power Macintosh 72xx, 75xx, 76xx, 82xx, 85xx, 95xx, PCI clones

  • MACH Version: 3.0
  • Linux Version: 2.0.23
  • Disk Space required: minimum 400 MB; recommended minimum 500 MB
  • RAM required: minimum 8 MB; recommended minimum 16 MB
  • SCSI: Disk & CDROM; Jaz drives and (possibly) other removables
  • Serial: DMA & Modem Control supported
  • Floppy: Not Yet. (Coming soon!)
  • Audio: Not Yet. (Coming soon!)
  • Video: Motherboard Video or Video Card including A/V and HPV card
    No multiple screen support, yet.
    Up to Millions of colors.
  • X Window System: X11R6.3
    1, 8, and 24 bit pixel depth support
    No support yet for 16 bit
  • Mouse support: 3-button mice and trackballs conforming to Apple's
    ADB protocol
    1-button mice use keyboard to emulate 2nd and 3rd buttons.
  • Ethernet: Yes
  • TCP/IP: Ethernet and PPP only
  • AppleTalk: supported over Ethernet via netatalk
  • SLIP/PPP: supported
  • NFS: supported
  • HFS: Native file system support is provided
