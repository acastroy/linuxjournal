Product Information

Manufacturer: Hewlett-Packard
URL: www.hp.com/workstations/products/linux/x4000/summary.html
Price: $6,859 US

The Good

  • Extreme performance with no configuration nightmares. (Previously, building a Linux system out at the edge of the performance envelope was difficult.)
  • High-performance in processor speed, bandwidth, 2-D and 3-D graphics.

The Bad

  • As a graphics workstation, it is still limited by lack of some tools (e.g., Adobe Photoshop) not being ported to Linux.
  • Extreme performance carries a high price tag.
