Product Information

Manufacturer: Benchmark
URL:www.4benchmark.com
Price: $1,449 MSRP US

The Good

  • Aggressively priced.
  • Space saving design for rackmount-optimized environments.
  • 640 Blade product offers a lot of storage in a small package.
  • Quiet and low power consumption.
  • Solid and reliable backup media used (DLT).

The Bad

  • Speed is reasonable but not stellar.
  • Worrisome noise when inserting and ejecting tapes.
