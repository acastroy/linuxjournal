Acknowledgments

ImageLinks, Inc. (a division of AGIS Advanced Geographic Information Systems) has been in business since 1996 and is a commercial spin-off venture of Harris Corporation. ImageLinks, Inc. is a center for the development and production of image analysis products and services.

Mark Lucas (mlucas@imagelinks.com), Chief Technical Officer of AGIS, is a founder of ImageLinks, Inc. Mr. Lucas is responsible for managing the development of the company's proprietary image processing system and leading the efforts to unite the remote sensing community in order to provide high-quality, easy-to-use data to their clients and the world at large. Mr. Lucas holds a BS degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Arizona and an MS degree in Computer Science from West Coast University in Santa Barbara, CA.
