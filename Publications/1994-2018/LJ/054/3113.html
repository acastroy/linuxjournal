Pacific Beach Invasion

LJ News Desk

Issue #54, October 1998

Possible penguin sighting confuses locals.

On July 5, the small town of Pacific Beach, Washington was invaded by a bird many thought to be a penguin, but was later identified as a “murre” (a common bird of northern seas).
