Resources

Primary Netatalk web page: http://www.umich.edu/~rsug/netatalk/

Other URLs for Netatalk: http://thehamptons.com/anders/netatalk/, and http://artoo.hitchcock.org/~flowerpt/projects/linux-netatalk/

While Netatalk provides you with the ability to mount the Linux file system on the Mac, hfs allows you to mount Macintosh hard drives, floppies and even CD-ROMs on your Linux system. You can find Information on this application at: http://www-sccm.stanford.edu/Students/hargrove/HFS/.

A moderated Netatalk mailing list which provides a general discussion of Netatalk and new releases is at netatalk-admins@umich.edu. Subscribe or unsubscribe by sending e-mail to netatalk-admins-request@umich.edu.

“MkLinux, Linux Comes to the Power Macintosh”, Richard C. S. Kinne, Linux Journal, November 1997.

“How to Build a Mac”, Andreas Schiffler and David Moody, Linux Journal, November 1995.
