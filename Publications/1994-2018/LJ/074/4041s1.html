We Talk to Everybody

Short items about people who contributed to kernel 1.0, and a few others involved with Linux at that early time. Learn what they are doing now, and how they feel about Linux and the Open Source movement. With three very different authors, see if you can figure out who wrote what.
by Marjorie Richardson, Jason Schumaker and David Penn

My Life with Linux

Jon tells us the story of his life and how he came to be involved with Linux and met Linus Torvalds. He forgot to mention what good friends they are, and how he is the godfather of Linus' children. He also gives us his views on how the Linux community can expand to include commercial interests while remaining true to spirit of open source.
by Jon “maddog” Hall
