Czechoslovakian-Naming Conventions

czechoslovak: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention of identificators.

accessczechoslovak: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention for abstract data types.

czechoslovakfcns: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention for functions and iterator names.

czechoslovakmacros: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention for all expanded macros.

czechoslovakvars: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention for variables.

czechoslovakconsts: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention for constants.

czechoslovaktypes: used for activating Czechoslovakian-naming convention for types.
