ext2tools are available in zipped format (ext2tool.zip) on sunsite.unc.edu in /pub/Linux/system/Filesystem/ext2 as well as on the numerous mirror sites. The simplest way to get it is through the Virtual Software Library at c/net central, http://www.cnet.com, where you can scan archive/mirror sites for the software and the operating system you want. This service is free, but you do have to register.

Some Unix tools for Windows95 can made life in the DOS/Windows world a little more comfortable. Look for Unix95.zip in one of the following archives:

ftp.cica.indiana.edu
ftp.marcam.com
ftp.cdrom.com
