Megahedron Pros

  • On-line documentation is provided.
  • Supports object import of various formats through its “extensibility”.
  • Wireframe modes support a form of hidden line removal that cleans up the wireframe displays.
  • Supports 3D stereo anaglyph rendering (red and blue stereo pairs).
  • Collision detection for animation.
  • License is fairly unrestrictive—one user, many machines. Not freely redistributable, but that's expected and acceptable with commercial packages.
  • Capable of network-based rendering.
  • Matrix math seems fairly easy to use:
v1 dot v2 = vector 1 dot product with vector 2
