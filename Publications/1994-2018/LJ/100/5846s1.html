Product Information



ImageStream Internet Solutions, Inc.
www.imagestream.com/Rebel.html
Rebel Router: $1,999 US
1-Port DS3 Card: $1,527 US
4-Port T1 Card: $2,705 US
Total: $6,231 US

The Good

  • Size: 1U rackmount.
  • Price: incredible flexibility and functionality at a much lower price than comparable routers.
  • Processing power and throughput: BGP-4 routing with DS3 capability—that says it all.
  • Security: SSH, ipchains, iptables and FreeS/WAN.
  • Support: rock-solid, very knowledgeable support staff combined with free software upgrades for the life of the product (I feel like I am in Wonderland).

The Bad

  • Manual (or rather lack thereof): there is not nearly as much documentation for this router as for a Cisco, partly because if you know Linux you don't need most of it. Basic documentation can be found on-line at support.imagestream.com/Manuals.html.
