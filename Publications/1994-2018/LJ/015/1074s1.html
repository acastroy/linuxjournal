Most of the pixmaps needed for xfm are supplied with the distribution. However, there are others available—maybe already on your system—poke around. Also, some others can be found at ftp.x.org in R5contrib/xpm3icons.tar.Z, or you can make some with xpaint.

xless, a file viewer, is available at sunsite in /pub/Linux/X11/xutils.
