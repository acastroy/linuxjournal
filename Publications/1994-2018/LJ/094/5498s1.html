Product Information


iXsystems, Inc.
www.ixsystems.net
Dual 1GHz Pentium III processor, 133MHz front-side bus, 1GB RAM, three 9.1GB Ultra3 10,000RPM SCSI hard drives, Ultra3 SCSI RAID controller, two 10/100 Ethernet ports, one PCI slot (occupied), Red Hat Linux 7.1
$3,319 US

The Good

  • Excellent value, even cheaper than Dell.
  • Leading-edge technology, including 1GHz Pentium III, Ultra3 SCSI RAID.
  • Red Hat Linux nicely installed, ready to roll.

The Bad

  • Cover, PCI slots should not use screws.
  • Should have either two PCI slots or RAID on the motherboard.
  • Operating system support rather pricey.
